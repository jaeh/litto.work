Mag. Daniela Weiss, *1984 Vienna, living artist specialised in AR
since 2017 Digital Art, Ruth Schnell, University of Applied Arts, Vienna (A) Artistic Direction from Artificial Museum

Statement

I construct installative objects, in the form of interactive VR and AR experiences, that turn the beholder into the performer.
From my perspective, these objects are virtual as well as physical references that make it impossible to distinguish between real, virtual and imaginary realities.
The mirror is a continuous symbol in my work.
