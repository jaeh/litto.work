Characters of mutation

I am curious what people in my surrounding think about headlong virtual changes in 2021, so I started a research in the 17th district in Vienna. This work is focused on the idea of blending two dimensions – fiction and reality – into a given information medium, derived from neighbours’ prognoses about the digital age. The visual outcome of my research is an in-between world, where virtual entities occupy the realm of urban space, translated through an augmented reality experience.

Mag. Daniela Weiss, *1984 Vienna, living artist specialised in AR
since 2017 Digital Art, Ruth Schnell, University of Applied Arts, Vienna (A) Artistic Direction from Artificial Museum

Statement

I construct installative objects, in the form of interactive VR and AR experiences, that turn the beholder into the performer.
From my perspective, these objects are virtual as well as physical references that make it impossible to distinguish between real, virtual and imaginary realities.
The mirror is a continuous symbol in my work.
